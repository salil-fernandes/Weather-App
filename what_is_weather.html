What is Weather exactly?

Weather is the state of the atmosphere, including temperature, atmospheric pressure, wind, humidity, precipitation, and cloud cover. It differs from climate, which is all weather conditions for a particular location averaged over about 30 years. Weather is influenced by latitude, altitude, and local and regional geography. One of the most significant impacts of weather is its influence on our choice of clothing. Weather forecasting is the application of science and technology to predict the state of the atmosphere for a future time and a given location. Common instruments used in weather forecasting include :

  • Hygrometer: Measures air humidity levels
  • Anemometer: Measures wind speed and direction
  • Barometer: Measures atmospheric pressure
  • Rain Gauge: Measures precipitation levels
